What is Blazing Stumps?

Blazing Stumps 2025 is the ultimate Under-19 interschool cricket tournament, where the fiercest young talents of Bangalore take to the pitch with fiery passion and school pride.

🏫 Host

CMR National Public School, Bangalore — a hub for talent, discipline, and leadership.

🏟️ Venue

CMR University Grounds — world-class facilities, high-octane atmosphere.

🎯 Eligibility

Open to all Under-19 school teams across Bangalore. Bring your A-game!
