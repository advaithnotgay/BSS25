What is Blazing Stumps?

Blazing Stumps 2025 is a prestigious Under-19 interschool cricket tournament, hosted by CMR NPS Bangalore and held at CMR University. It brings together top school teams from across Bangalore to compete, connect, and celebrate the spirit of cricket.
